Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Coursera Assistant Pro handles information when you use the Chrome extension and this website.

Information handled by the extension

The extension may read visible course page content only to provide its features, such as quiz assistance, discussion drafting, transcript extraction, reading extraction, and study chat. This may include page text, questions, answer options, discussion prompts, reading content, and transcript text shown on the page.

Local storage

The extension may store settings locally in your browser, including selected AI provider settings, usage counters, saved transcripts, saved reading material, and trial or activation status. Local data remains on your device unless you choose to send it to an AI provider through the extension's features.

AI providers

When you use AI-powered features, relevant prompts and course content may be sent to the AI provider selected in the extension settings. If you enter your own API key, that key is stored locally in your browser and is used only for requests to the provider you select.

Payments and license keys

Payments are processed by Dodo Payments or another checkout provider linked from this website. Payment details are handled by the payment provider, not by this static website. License key validation may require sending your license key and activation information to the license validation service.

What this website collects

This static GitHub Pages website does not intentionally collect personal information. GitHub or other hosting providers may process technical logs according to their own policies.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared only as needed to provide extension features, process payments, validate licenses, comply with law, or protect the product from misuse.

User responsibility

You are responsible for reviewing AI-generated output and for following the rules of your course, institution, platform, or workplace.
